About

Brendon Shapiro


Brendon Shapiro is a versatile pianist, vocal coach, and conductor with a specialty in opera and a passion for contemporary music. He has enjoyed working for numerous companies including Boston Lyric Opera, Opera NEO, Boston Opera Collaborative, MetroWest Opera, Opera Fayetteville, North End Music and Performing Arts Center, Opera on Tap, and Juventas New Music Ensemble.

Brendon is currently serving as a staff pianist at Boston University, the organist for First Parish in Arlington, MA, and the assistant music director for the Boston Saengerfest Men's Chorus. He has also been on faculty as a vocal coach at the Boston University Tanglewood Institute. As a freelance pianist, Brendon is frequently sought out for recitals, competitions, concerts, auditions, recording sessions, and coachings.

Brendon has enjoyed working outside of the classical music scene as well. He served as musical director for the first four seasons of the newly renovated Woodstock Playhouse in Woodstock, NY. In a performance praised by the Boston Globe, he appeared in a production of Master Class with the New Repertory Theatre portraying Manny, the onstage pianist. In addition, he has performed as a featured soloist with Video Games Live with whom he also recorded a solo piano album, Through Time and Space, which became a #1 bestseller on Amazon.com.

Brendon holds a Master of Music degree in Collaborative Piano Performance from Boston University and a Bachelor of Music degree from the Ithaca College School of Music where he won the Mary Hayes North Piano Competition Award.

Services

Vocal Coaching

$60/hour

Brendon is an experienced vocal coach of opera, art song, and musical theatre, and is comfortable working with singers of all ages. He can help you bring a piece to life with a strong focus on natural and clear delivery of the text and attention to stylistic detail. Brendon typically coaches out of Boston University or from his home in Malden, MA.

Recital

$250 – $300

Brendon would love to be your collaborative partner for your upcoming recital! Check out the media section for some samples of his performances. Fee will vary slightly depending on difficulty/quantity of repertoire and number of rehearsals requested.

Audio/Video Recording

$55/hour

Brendon is frequently hired to help make recordings for pre-screenings, competitions, applications, websites, etc... he is familiar with the standard repertoire of opera, art song, and musical theatre, and is a pretty quick study when it comes to learning new music. Sending music at least a week in advance is always a good idea!

Audition/Competition

$50

Brendon has a good deal of experience playing for auditions and competitions of all kinds and excels at calming neurotic musicians down before they have to walk out on stage! Brendon may charge a small additional fee if it's a bit of a journey to your audition, or if you get a callback/make it to the next round of your competition!

Voice Lesson Accompaniment

$45/hour

Need someone to play for your upcoming voice lesson? Look no further! Brendon may charge a small additional fee if it's a bit of a journey to your voice lesson.

Miscellaneous

Brendon is also happy to consider playing for your musical theatre or operatic production, choral concert, wedding, bar mitzvah, birthday party, or pretty much anything else you could name. Don't hesistate to reach out with any inquiries—please visit the contact section!
